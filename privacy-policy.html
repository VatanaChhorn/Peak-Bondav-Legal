This Privacy Policy explains how the Peak Bondav team (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Peak Bondav mobile application (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Overview

Peak Bondav is a word puzzle game. We aim to collect as little personal information as possible. Most data is processed to keep the App working, to show ads, and to improve your experience. We do not sell your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Device & usage data: device model, operating system version, language, app version, crash logs, and general usage statistics.
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA): used to deliver and measure ads, only with your permission.
  • Game data: your progress, scores, and settings stored on your device and, where you opt in, in Apple Game Center.

We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email, or phone number to play.

3. How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the App and its features.
  • Save and sync your game progress and preferences.
  • Display advertising and measure its performance.
  • Diagnose technical issues and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Advertising & Tracking

The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. With your consent, AdMob and its partners may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to show personalized ads and measure their effectiveness. On first launch, iOS will ask for your permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt.

You can change your choice at any time in Settings › Privacy & Security › Tracking on your device. If you decline tracking, you will still see ads, but they will be less personalized. Learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com.

5. Third-Party Services

The App relies on third-party services that may process data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Game Center & App Store — game services. Privacy Policy

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers (such as ad and analytics partners) who process it on our behalf.
  • When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or us.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you.

7. Data Retention

Game progress and settings are stored on your device for as long as the App is installed. Data processed by third-party services is retained according to their own policies. You can delete most local data by uninstalling the App.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

10. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to opt out of personalized advertising. You can:

  • Manage ad tracking in your device's Settings.
  • Reset your advertising identifier in iOS Settings.
  • Uninstall the App to stop further data collection.
  • Contact us to exercise any applicable data rights.

11. International Users

The App may be used worldwide. Information may be processed in countries other than your own, including by our third-party providers, where data protection laws may differ. By using the App, you consent to such processing.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
